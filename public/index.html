Last One Wins

Last person to enter before time runs out wins everything.

  • Send any amount of tokens to enter the game
  • Every entry resets the countdown and grows the pot
  • When the timer hits zero, the last person to enter wins the entire pot
  • ⚡ Speed Up: spend 100 tokens to jump into the lead and reset the clock to a fresh 30 minutes — force a fast showdown
  • 🔥 Win Streaks: win consecutive rounds for a bonus on top of the pot — the longer the streak, the bigger the reward
  • A new round starts automatically — jump back in!

Timing is everything. Enter too early and risk getting replaced. Enter too late and miss your chance.

All game state lives on the Usernode blockchain. The server tracks the timer and sends automated payouts. Tap anywhere outside this panel to close.
